NURSEBOT

The Automated NurseBot is a compact, multifunctional machine designed to assist in routine patient check-ups by measuring vital signs such as height, weight, and body temperature. It features a number queuing system and prints data slips, helping to streamline patient flow and reduce workload for nurses. The NurseBot is a cost-effective, user-friendly innovation developed to improve healthcare service efficiency, particularly in clinics and resource-limited settings.

FAQ'S

What is the weight limit that the NurseBot can measure?
The NurseBot is designed to accurately measure patients up to 200 kilograms. It may not provide precise readings beyond this limit.
What is the height range the NurseBot can measure?
The NurseBot can measure individuals ranging from 6 ft and not below 4 ft in height.
Does the NurseBot store patient data?
The NurseBot does not permanently store patient data unless integrated with a hospital's database system. For standalone use, data is processed temporarily for receipt generation and is not saved.
How long does it take for the NurseBot to complete a full check-up per patient?
On average, it takes 2–3 minutes per patient for the NurseBot to complete all vital sign measurements and provide a printed receipt or priority number.
What is the main purpose of the NurseBot?
The NurseBot is designed to streamline routine patient check-up tasks such as measuring vital signs (weight, height, and temperature) assigning priority numbers to improve patient flow and reduce waiting time in healthcare facilities.
The push button is unresponsive. What should I check first?
Inspect for loose or detached wires and verify if the connected microcontroller is powered on.
Can I perform maintenance without technical experience?
Basic maintenance like wire checking and cleaning can be done by operators, but internal issues or replacements should be handled by technical personnel.
